Overviews
  • The Mountain Is You by Brianna Wiest is a powerful book about self-sabotage, emotional healing, and personal transformation. It explores the reasons why we hold ourselves back and provides practical strategies to overcome internal struggles, break negative patterns, and unlock our full potential. Wiest uses the metaphor of a mountain to represent our personal challenges, emphasizing that the only way forward is to climb, transform, and grow. Through deep insights on self-awareness, emotional resilience, and change, this book helps readers build a stronger, more fulfilling life.
